There are many animals in need of food and shelter. You should consider donating to pet charities!



Here are some great charities. Donate Today!



The Animal Welfare Institute. 96 percent of every dollar donated goes directly to providing care for stray animals, fighting against laws that hurt wildlife, and spreading awareness of animal plights around the world.



American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (ASPCA). Provides shelter, medical care, food, and even training to animals in need throughout the country.



Friends of Animals. The most successful breeding prevention programs in America. One of the few organizations that works on rescuing feral animals, improving laws for animal welfare, and more.



D.E.L.T.A Rescue. Giving shelter to over 1,500 different animals, including a total of 800 dogs and a huge swath of cats as well.



PetSmart Charities. PetSmart Charities has helped facilitate 10 percent of all pet adoptions in America. That's around five million happy homes!



The American Humane Association. Ever since 1877, the American Humane Association has been working to ensure a better life for both pets and wildlife alike. The AHA has also stepped in to save children and domestic violence victims from abuse in the past as well.
